DIY Instant Coffee

Ingredients

  • Milk powder        4 tbsp
  • Coffee powder    1 tsp
  • Sugar powder    4 tsp
  • Hot water           2 cups

**The above mentioned measures are for only two servings

Method

In a bowl add milk powder, coffee powder and sugar powder and mix them well. Put exactly half of the prepared mixture in each cup. Add hot water and stir well. Instant Coffee is ready! enjoy it with cookies, biscuits, your favourite snack, or it alone can make you feel fresh.
