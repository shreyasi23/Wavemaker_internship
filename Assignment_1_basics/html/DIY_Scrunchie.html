DIY Scrunchie

Material Required

  • Cloth 25 cm
  • Elastic 20 cm
  • Sissors
  • Needle and thread or a sewing machine
  • Straight pins 2-3
  • Safety pin 1
  • Iron

Method

  1. Fold the cloth into half along the length.
  2. Secure it with straight pins and iron it.
  3. Stich the cloth along the length
  4. Attach the safety pin to one of the ends of the elastic and then insert it inside the cloth from one end.
  5. Pull the pin out the from other end. Remove the saftey pin and tie both the ends of the elastic.
  6. Cover the elastic by overlapping the ends of the cloth and secure it by stiching the ends.

Your cute scrunchie is ready to make your ponytail look beautiful!
